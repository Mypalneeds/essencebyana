Frequently Asked Questions

Navigate to the "My Mentor" section and click "Schedule Session" to view your mentor's available times. Select a time slot and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with meeting details.

Visit the "My Progress" section to see your completed tasks, current level, and upcoming milestones. You can also view your goal tracker on the Overview page.

If you feel you need a different mentor, please submit a support ticket explaining your situation. Our admin team will review your request and help facilitate a re-pairing if appropriate.

Login streaks count consecutive days you access the platform. Task completion streaks track tasks completed before their deadline. Maintain your streaks to unlock special badges and achievements!

The Prayer Wall allows you to share prayer requests with the community and support others. Click "Share Request" to post your own, or click "Praying" on others' requests to let them know you're praying for them.
